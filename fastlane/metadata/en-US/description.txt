Take control of your finances with Moovy — the simple and powerful personal finance app designed to help you track every dollar in and out.

TRACK INCOME & EXPENSES
Easily log your income and expenses, set due dates, and manage recurring payments across multiple months. Moovy gives you a clear monthly overview of your financial health.

MONTHLY OVERVIEW
See your total income, total expenses, and balance at a glance. Navigate between months to review past movements and plan ahead.

MULTI-CURRENCY SUPPORT
Use Moovy in your local currency — supports USD, EUR, BRL, DKK, CNY, JPY, KRW, and more.

AVAILABLE IN 8 LANGUAGES
Moovy speaks your language: English, Portuguese, Spanish, German, Danish, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean.

THEMES
Choose between light, dark, or system theme to match your preference.

SECURE SIGN-IN
Sign in with Apple or Google to keep your data safe and synced.

Moovy is built for people who want a clean, no-nonsense way to understand where their money goes every month.
